Welcome to the Riddle Page

  1. The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?

    2. Footsteps

  2. Poor people have it. Rich people need it. If you eat it you die. What is it?

    3. Nothing

  3. I'm tall when I'm young and I'm short when I'm old. What am I?

    4. Candle

  4. What travels around the world but stays in one spot?

    5. A stamp

  5. What has hands but can not clap?

    6. A clock

  6. What is so delicate that saying its name breaks it?

    7. Silence

  7. What is as big as you are and yet does not weigh anything?

    8. Your shadow

  8. Paul's height is six feet, he's an assistant at a butcher's shop, and wears size 9 shoes. What does he weigh?

    9. Meat

  9. What has many keys, but can't even open a single door?

    10. A piano

  10. I come right in front of Earth, at the end of time, and twice in a week. What am I?

    11. The letter E
